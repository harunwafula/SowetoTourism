Background

Soweto Tourism Re- established in 2017 by the local tourism businesses in Soweto who recognized the need to have an organized vehicle that will serve as their mouth piece; channel of communication between private and public sector (PUBLIC PRIVATE PARTNERSHIP model). The Association is the voice of the tourism SMME’s and community in Soweto.

The Local Tourism Association (LTA) is a membership based organization; fully registered and compliant with the laws of the Republic. We currently have a membership base of +100 registered SMME members and growing. Our operation space is currently at Ubuntu kraal which is strategically placed and earmarked by our strategic partners to become a landing space for tourists visiting Soweto on a daily.

10 Power Objectives

  1. Promote Soweto Tourism products.
  2. Facilitate market access: through local and international exhibitions; and other platforms.
  3. Facilitate business linkage opportunities and funding.
  4. Improve product and service excellence.
  5. Promote and practice responsible tourism.
  6. Promote and market Soweto as a safe, friendly and welcoming environment.
  7. Facilitate skills development for members through other creditable institutions.
  8. Develop and Market Soweto as a tourist destination of choice in the country.
  9. Create sustainable and quantifiable jobs in tourism sector.
  10. Ensure successful and sustainability of the tourism sector.

Investment Conferrence

The Soweto Investment conference is a high level destination development project initiated by Soweto Tourism and Business Development Association working committee together with strategic partners. The Aim of the investment conference is to attract local and international investors and partners,who are interested in partnering towards creating and developing Soweto in to an environment that is economically sustainable, business friendly, ecologically welcoming, connected to the globe through the internet and other forms and most importantly attractive to the local, regional and international tourist market to drive direct and indirect foreign capital investment. Nasrec Expo Centre has partnered with Soweto Tourism in hosting this 1st of its kind in the countries Township Economic development vision.
